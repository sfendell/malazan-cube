The Lord and the Lady
Cost: 2UR
Type: Legendary Creature - God
P/T: 4/4

Whenever you scry, if you put more cards on top, put a Pull counter on this. Otherwise, put a Push counter on this. If there are more Push counters than Pull counters on this, creatures you control get +X/+0, where X is the difference. Otherwise, creatures you don't control get -X/-0. At the beginning of your end step, if this has fewer than 7 counters on it, flip a coin. If you lose the flip, target opponent gains control of this.
