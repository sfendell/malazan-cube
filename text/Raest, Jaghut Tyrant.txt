Raest, Jaghut Tyrant
Cost: 5UB
Type: Legendary Creature - Elder Orc
P/T: 6/6

Whenever Raest attacks alone, freeze X creatures, where X is 1 plus the number of creatures you control that you do not own. At the end of your turn, gain control of target creature with a stun counter on it for as long as you control Raest. Sacrifice three creatures you do not own: Raest ascends. Activate this ability only once per game.
