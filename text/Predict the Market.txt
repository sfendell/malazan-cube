Predict the Market
Cost: 1UB
Type: Kindred Enchantment - Merchant Aura

Enchant player
Whenever a permanent leaves the battlefield, put a derivative counter on this. At the beginning of your upkeep, if this has fewer counters than enchanted player controls, remove a counter from this and lose 1 life. Otherwise, sacrifice this and draw X cards, where X is the number of counters on it.
