Barghast Exhumation
Cost: R
Type: Instant

Kicker G, kicker W Deal 2 damage to any target. If you paid G, up to one target creature you control fights target creature you don’t control. If you paid W, gain life equal to noncombat damage dealt this turn. If you paid GW, destroy up to one target artifact or enchantment if its mana value is less than how much life you’ve gained this turn.
