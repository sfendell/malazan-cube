T’lan Ascendancy
Cost: BRG
Type: Kindred Enchantment - Elder Zombie

Elder Zombies you control get +1/+1. Slow, BRG, Sacrifice a land: Deal 2 damage to any target. Whenever a land you control is put into your graveyard from anywhere, cook a 2/2 black, red, and green Elder Zombie creature token with reach.
