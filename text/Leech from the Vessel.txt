Leech from the Vessel
Cost: 2B
Type: Enchantment - Aura

Enchant creature. At the beginning of your main phase, you may put a -1/-1 counter on target creature you control. If you do, put X +1/+1 counters on enchanted creature, where X is the number of -1/-1 counters on that creature. Sacrifice this: Remove all -1/-1 counters from creatures you control.
